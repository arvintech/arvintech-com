## Core Services

### 1. AI Readiness Assessment
**What it is:** Comprehensive evaluation of your current technology, processes, and team capabilities.

**Deliverables:**
- Current state analysis
- Gap identification
- Risk assessment
- Opportunity mapping
- Readiness score (1-100)

**Duration:** 2-4 weeks

---

### 2. Strategic Roadmap Development
**What it is:** Custom 12-24 month plan to achieve AI readiness.

**Deliverables:**
- Prioritized initiative list
- Timeline with milestones
- Budget estimates
- Resource requirements
- Quick wins identification

**Duration:** 2-3 weeks

---

### 3. Technology Stack Evaluation
**What it is:** Review of existing tools and recommendations for AI-compatible alternatives.

**Areas covered:**
- CRM and customer data
- Operations and workflow tools
- Communication platforms
- Data storage and management
- Security infrastructure

**Deliverables:**
- Stack audit report
- Integration recommendations
- Migration priorities
- Cost-benefit analysis

---

### 4. Data Foundation Building
**What it is:** Preparing your data for AI utilization.

**Services:**
- Data audit and cleanup
- Structure standardization
- Quality improvement
- Governance framework
- Privacy compliance review

**Why it matters:** AI is only as good as the data it learns from.

---

### 5. Team Readiness & Training
**What it is:** Preparing your people for the AI-augmented workplace.

**Components:**
- Leadership AI literacy workshops
- Department-specific training
- Change management support
- Champion identification and development
- Ongoing learning paths

---

### 6. Implementation Support
**What it is:** Hands-on guidance during AI tool adoption.

**Services:**
- Vendor selection assistance
- Pilot program design
- Integration oversight
- Performance monitoring
- Course correction

## Engagement Models

### Discovery Session (Free)
- 30-minute call
- Understand your challenges
- High-level recommendations
- Fit assessment

### Clarity Sprint (2 weeks)
- Rapid assessment
- Top 3 priorities identified
- 90-day action plan
- Investment: $5,000-$10,000

### Full Clarity Program (8-12 weeks)
- Comprehensive assessment
- Full roadmap development
- Implementation kickoff
- Quarterly check-ins (Year 1)
- Investment: $25,000-$75,000

### Ongoing Advisory
- Monthly strategy sessions
- Continuous optimization
- New opportunity identification
- Investment: $2,500-$5,000/month
